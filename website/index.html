emDEX is a FrameMaker™ Plug-In, adding a real-time visual means of watching your index grow right before your eyes to help you track and modify your index entries on the fly. No more need to tag-and-generate again and again.

 

      The emDEX advantage provides:

  • sizeable marker edit window with user-defined formatting buttons, font control, and auto-completion of marker text
  • real-time visual updating of the emDEX index window AS YOU ARE MARKING ENTRIES, allowing you to view and modify entries as you go
  • real-time formatting of locators and "See", "See also" references
  • visual index creation for single documents or books
  • global search and replace
  • one-step range marking by highlighting text
  • repeat last marker button
   
 
The demo version of emDEX has all the capabilities of the full program but is limited to 100 entries.
